Bruce Banner

Dr. Bruce Banner lives a life caught between the soft-spoken scientist he's always been and the uncontrollable green monster powered in his rage.


Powers

Exposed to heavy doses of gamma radiation, scientist Bruce Banner transforms into the mean, green rage machine called the Hulk.

Superhuman Strength

The Hulk’s strength is immense, allowing him to stop foes even bigger than he is with a single blow, to throw massively heavy objects, and to tear apart metal as though it were paper.

Long Distance Jumping

The Hulk’s leg muscles are strong enough to allow him to leap great distances in a short period of time.

Hulk Smash

Slamming his hands together, the Hulk can create a massive concussive wave.

Durability and Regeneration

The Hulk’s skin is able to withstand a tremendous amount of injury and he is able to heal far quicker than a normal human when harmed.

Genius Intelligence

Bruce Banner is among Earth’s most brilliant men, leading to remarkable (if not always positive) creations like Ultron alongside Tony Stark.
